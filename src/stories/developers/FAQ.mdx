### The Short Answer

DDS pages are designed with configuration in mind. Components follow standards and best practices,
covering 99% of common use cases. For truly unique requirements, we have clear patterns.

### How Configuration Works

DDS uses **callback presence** and **predicates** for configuration - not boolean flag explosion.
