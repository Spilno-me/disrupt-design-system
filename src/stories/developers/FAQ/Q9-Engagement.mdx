## Summary

<div style={{
  display: 'grid',
  gridTemplateColumns: 'repeat(2, 1fr)',
  gap: '16px',
  marginBottom: '24px',
}}>
  <GuidelineCard type="do" title="Use the right channel">
    Critical = Slack DM. Normal = #dds-support. Feature requests = GitHub Issue.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard type="do" title="Propose shared needs">
    Multi-consumer requests get prioritized. Rally other teams if you want something built.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard type="dont" title="Force design exceptions">
    Consistency is the point. Build one-offs yourself, don't compromise DDS patterns.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard type="dont" title="Expect DDS to translate">
    DDS renders your content. Translations are your responsibility.
  </GuidelineCard>
</div>
