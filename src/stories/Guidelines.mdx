### Logo Do's and Don'ts

<div style={{
  display: 'grid',
  gridTemplateColumns: 'repeat(2, 1fr)',
  gap: '20px',
  marginBottom: '48px',
}}>
  <GuidelineCard
    type="do"
    title="Use approved logo files"
    image="/logos/D-pixels-dark-full.svg"
  >
    Always use the official logo files from the brand assets. Never recreate or modify the logo.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard
    type="dont"
    title="Stretch or distort"
  >
    Never stretch, compress, or distort the logo. Always maintain the original aspect ratio.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard
    type="do"
    title="Use appropriate contrast"
  >
    Use the dark logo on light backgrounds and the light logo on dark backgrounds.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard
    type="dont"
    title="Add effects"
  >
    Never add shadows, gradients, outlines, or other effects to the logo.
  </GuidelineCard>
</div>

<h2 style={{ marginTop: SPACING.px.sectionHeadingTop, marginBottom: SPACING.px.sectionHeadingBottom }}>Color Usage</h2>

<SectionHeader
  icon={<Palette size={20} />}
  title="Brand Colors in Context"
  description="How to apply brand colors correctly across different use cases."
/>

<div style={{
  display: 'grid',
  gridTemplateColumns: 'repeat(2, 1fr)',
  gap: '20px',
  marginBottom: '32px',
}}>
  <GuidelineCard type="do" title="Use semantic colors">
    Apply colors based on their semantic meaning. Teal for interactive elements, dark for text, purple for accents.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard type="dont" title="Use colors randomly">
    Don't apply brand colors arbitrarily. Each color has a purpose in the design system.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard type="do" title="Maintain contrast ratios">
    Ensure text meets WCAG AA standards: 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text.
  </GuidelineCard>

  <GuidelineCard type="dont" title="Use low contrast combinations">
    Avoid light text on light backgrounds or dark text on dark backgrounds.
  </GuidelineCard>
</div>
